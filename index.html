About Me

I am an aspiring front end web developer born and raised in New Jersey. I attended Rutgers University and graduated with a degree in Information Technology and Informatics in 2013. My first major career position was at the Federal Reserve System where I began my career as a database administrator. While at the Fed, I transferred to the Dallas office for a change of pace and continue to live in Dallas today.

I switched organizations to GM Financial where I continued to improve my data analyst skills until I was recently laid off in May 2023. At this point I realized it was a good opportunity to improve my skills. I had always been fascinated with development work but only casually learned in my spare time. I am currently enrolled at the SMU coding boot camp and am learning HTML, CSS, and Javascript and hope to continue utilizing this skillset in another organization.
